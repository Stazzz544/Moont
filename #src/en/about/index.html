Brand Moont

Unique clothes with hand-made embroidery – no two equal things exist as you never find two twin branches on a tree.

Brand Moont was established by Olga Volkova, Designer from Berlin, Germany. Olga sees her mission in creating beautiful things and transforming stunning beauty of nature into everyday wear. The idea is not new: it correlates with the Japanese tradition Ukiyo – ‘pictures of the floating world’ and refers to transient everyday life.

Most of the clothes are produced in the Tuyana Irincheeva fashion house. The pieces of clothing are very cozy and ergonomic, models and materials made for life. You can put them into the washing machine and feel safe about the result.

Only ready pieces of clothing are usually sold.
